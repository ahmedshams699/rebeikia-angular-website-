About Rebeikia

Rebeikia is a joint stock company located in Cairo, Egypt. Proudly serving all business sectors to achieve the 2030 national sustainable development strategies of Egypt. We follow the highest international code for waste disposal. Being environmentally conscious and revolutionizing waste management. It is our goal to reform the current waste collection system in Egypt. With the active participation of giving back to the community, through green energy, and charity. Shaping the future.
