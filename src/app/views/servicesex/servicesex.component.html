Always Giving Back

Our community is the pillar that supports us and is inevitably the main driving force for our success which is why community engagement is an essential part of our plan, we aspire to achieve that through:

  • Industrial Training
  • Academic Engagement
  • Social Awareness

The government's support and the community’s awareness of our vision aid us by streamlining our path to achieve our mission and to take concrete steps towards reshaping Egypt’s and Rebekeia’s future. To make a real impact , we encourage communities to work collaboratively with Rebeikia to reach our goals and a more sustainable Egypt. Real change then comes from within the community and is rather seen as the community giving back to their country.
