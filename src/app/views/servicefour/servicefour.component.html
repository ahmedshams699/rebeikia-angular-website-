Eco-lectricity

Recycling comes in several forms and is beneficial in many ways. Converting non recyclable products to electricity to power our neighborhoods is an aligned goal with Egypt's Sustainable Development Goals  for 2030 and it is our goal to enable the government reach it. The trash collected can effectively be transformed into green usable electricity that is fed back into the main lines and used by all Egyptian houses seamlessly.
