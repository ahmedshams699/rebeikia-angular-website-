Return & Earn

We redefine the concept of municipality via our reward system which is one of its kind in the MENA region that actualizes an extra source of income to your business; through:

  • Fair Evaluation
  • Economic Leverage
  • Generating Revenue

The idea itself is simple. to collect the waste that is provided and separate the items depending on their irreconcilability, and then to provide the customer with an appropriate income that will be generated by recycling their waste, the simplicity of the idea does not take away from its importance towards taking Egypt to a better, cleaner tomorrow and ever so closer to our national 2030 vision.
